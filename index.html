Features

Private competitions

Invite your friends and colleagues to predict with you on the sports competition of your choice.
It's free, up to 40 participants per contest.
Beyond 40 people, contact us to benefit from the company offer.
Alternatively, join a public prognostic contest open to all.

Advanced customization

Define your point scale to be awarded in case of good or bad prognosis, and bonus points when the exact score of a match has been found.
Determine whether or not prognoses should include extra time and give a tolerance for exact scores on football matches.
Predict on the teams coming out of the pools, the best attack, the best defense and the winner of the competition to earn more points.

Live

Updated scores and live rankings after each goal.
Receive push notifications directly in your web browser or on your mobile to be alerted to the result.
Do not miss a prognosis: receive a summary of your notifications every day by e-mail for the events you have selected.
